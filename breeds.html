Popular Breeds at GRYWOODACRESSTABLE

Gypsy Cob

Heavy, feathered, and kind-tempered—great family horses, often used for driving and happy hacking.

Irish Sport Horse

Cross of Irish Draught and Thoroughbred—bold, athletic, and popular for eventing and all-round riding.

Highland Pony

Hardy native Scottish pony, known for strength, calm nature, and surefootedness—great for trekking and family use.

Warmblood

Athletic sport horse bred for disciplines like dressage and jumping—calmer than Thoroughbreds, popular in competitions.

Welsh Pony and Cob

Strong, flashy native breed with stamina and presence—versatile for riding, driving, and showing.

Thoroughbred

Fast, sensitive, and athletic—primarily bred for racing but often retrained for eventing and jumping.

Section A Pony

A small, hardy native pony known for its refined looks, intelligence, and great movement. Ideal for lead rein and first ridden classes—popular with children due to their size and character.
